Our Story

The idea of Heavenly Dates is a simple inspiration of indulgent and delightful gifting.

Our aim is to create a luxurious treat that burst with variety of flavours.

Each of our chocolate dates is handmade with care and passion to deliver a divine taste. We carefully handcrafted each date paying close attention to details to design a lavish array of goodness.

Our passion is gifting and we are keen to create an impressive gifts that leaves a pleasant long lasting impression.

We have set our standards high, and we are dedicated to deliver excellent quality products that meets high expectation.
